Hello my name is not Frank Dowdy but there is already a Michael Trent

This website is a response to Douglas Rushkoff's comments on the sinister nature of the way big tech companies mediate the communication between people. Rushkoff though something was lost when the internet became the World Wide Web. He thought easier didn't mean better.

Learning HTML turned out not to be that difficult (as Rushkoff said) and I spent an afternoon getting this site live. The site will probably change over time but I got my own text online in less than a day without Twitter or Facebook being involved.
