About

Hello there. My name in not really Frank Dowdy but there is already a Michael Trent.

I set up this webste because I wanted to make something one weekend and I thought about what Douglas Rushkoff said in an interview about HTML not being difficult to learn. Rushkoff is also critical of the way big tech companies mediate the communication between people. He thought something was lost when the internet became the World Wide Web. He thought easier didn't mean better.

Learning HTML turned out not to be that difficult and I spent an afternoon getting this site live. The site will probably change over time but I got my own text online in less than a day without Twitter or Facebook being involved.
